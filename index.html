Finch 3D — Turning an Unusable First Step into a Guided Flow

First-time users hit a hard stop: the tool demanded a “center line” with no definition, no example, and no way forward. I diagnosed the onboarding failure through competitive analysis and designed a 4-step guided flow—define the term, show the destination, place the first point, connect—cutting projected onboarding time by ~20%.
