About Medya

UX designer specializing in data-driven workflows and platform experiences for web and desktop. I lead full-cycle UX projects—from research through high-fidelity prototypes—and build code-based prototypes with AI-assisted tooling to close the gap between design and engineering. My architecture background gives me a systems-level lens for complex, multi-stakeholder products.
