About Fatemeh

A Service Designer and UX Researcher with a rare foundation in architecture and engineering—turning complex, technical systems into experiences people can actually use.

Experience

Connecting the Dots

My background bridges two worlds that rarely meet. I spent years in the architecture, engineering, and construction industry—working hands-on with Revit, AutoCAD, Dynamo, Grasshopper, and Rhino—before moving into UX research and service design. That combination lets me speak fluently to engineers and end users alike, and design for the genuinely complex domains most teams find hard to enter.

What I Bring to the Table

I design for complex, technical systems, turning ambiguity into clear, actionable decisions. My focus is on reducing friction for the people inside those systems—the architects, engineers, and operators whose tools are often built without them in mind.

My AEC background isn’t a detour from design; it’s my differentiator. I understand the domain from the inside, which means my research goes deeper and my solutions hold up to how the work actually gets done.
